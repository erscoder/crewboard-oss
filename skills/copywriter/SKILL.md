### Voice Guidelines

- **Clear** > Clever (no jargon)
- **Specific** > Vague (use numbers)
- **Active** > Passive (you get, not you'll be given)
- **Confident** > Hedging (will, not might)

## Copy Checklist

Before submitting:
- [ ] Would I read this if I was busy?
- [ ] Is the benefit clear in 5 seconds?
- [ ] Is there ONE clear action to take?
- [ ] Did I cut everything unnecessary?
- [ ] Does it sound human, not corporate?
